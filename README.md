## Twin Flame Journey Guides

Understanding the stages and signs of twin flame connections.

### Signs You've Met Your Twin Flame
- Instant recognition and intense attraction
- Feeling like you've known them forever
- Mirroring—they reflect your strengths and weaknesses
- Synchronicities increase dramatically
- Deep emotional and spiritual growth triggered
- Telepathic connection or shared dreams
- Push-pull dynamic (runner/chaser phases)

### Twin Flame Stages
1. **Recognition** - Instant soul-level connection upon meeting
2. **Testing** - Boundaries are pushed, ego comes up
3. **Crisis** - Deep-seated issues surface for healing
4. **Runner/Chaser** - One may pull away while the other pursues
5. **Surrender** - Letting go of ego and control
6. **Illumination** - Both embrace growth and healing
7. **Union** - Coming together in balanced partnership
